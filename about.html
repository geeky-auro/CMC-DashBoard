CountMyCrunch

CountMyCrunch, is an app which helps a desk to get well in a personal way of his/her life. It is an app which helps the user to built his/her unhealthy or boring Life to a amazing, perfect and healthy life.

Purpose

  • The CountMyCrunch Application helps to determine body fat based on height and weight that applies to adult men and women.
  • CountMyCrunch will also help to estimate the number of calories a person needs to consume each day.
  • CountMyCrunch will also provide some simple guidelines for gaining or losing weight.
  • CountMyCrunch will also provide data for all kinds of fruit! You can use FoodBie to find out interesting information about Recipes and educate yourself.
  • Finally it has a fitness tracking capability which will help users to keep track of recent calorie measurements used by our Application.

Main Tools of our App:

BMI Calculator:

Body mass index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height, age and weight that applies to adult men and women. IN this part of CountMyCrunch, the user can check his/her health's BMI. After selecting the gender,The user is only required to give input of it's age(in years), height(in foot and inches) and weight(in kg) for the calculation of his/her BMI. When the user will give all the required inputs for calculating the BMI and Submit it. Then the app will show the result to the user, whether he/she is "Underweight", "Normal Weight" or"Overweight"

Calorie Counter:

In this part of CountMyCrunch the Calorie counter will calculate, How much a user can intake his/her daily calories. After selecting the user's gender, the user can give the inputs required for calculating the calorie. Then you can select 'How much workout you are doing for your body?' which is presented below of the height input. After this you can select what are the foods you want to eat for making you healthy. For this you have to select the foods which are given below. By selecting the Plus sign '+' you can select the food which you want to intake as your daily health basis, and by selecting the Minus sign '-' you can decline the food which you don't want to eat but selected wrongly. Then after clicking on the calculate button the app will show you your total calorie intake which you have selected by selecting the food items. if you swipe to the right side of that you can see the daily estimated calorie need for a human according to its exercise routine. Then if you want to save your result details then you can save it by selecting the save button which is given below or cancel it by selecting the cancel button.

Foodie:

Type a Recipe name to get full information about a recipe, such as ingredients, nutrition, diet and allergen information, etc

In this part of CountMyCrunch you can track your fitness progression. After saving your fitness details in calorie counter of CountMyCrunch, you can open the calorie tracker part to see the saved progression.
